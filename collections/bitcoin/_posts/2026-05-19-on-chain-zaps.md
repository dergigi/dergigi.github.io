[NIP-57] gave nostr zaps. Lightning zaps. Zap requests, zap receipts, lud16 in your profile, the whole circus. It worked well enough that "zap" became a verb on nostr the way "like" became a verb on Twitter.

But Lightning zaps are Lightning zaps. On-chain is different. Different tradeoffs. Different UX. Different failure modes. Different privacy properties. Different infrastructure assumptions.

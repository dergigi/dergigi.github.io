Hi, I'm Gigi.

Most people know me as the author of Bitcoin is Time and 21 Lessons. Some know me as the guy who says "GM" on nostr a lot.

I used to be a software engineer. What I am now is unclear to me, at least at this very moment. Things are in flux. LLMs are doing most of the coding these days, so my usual go-to introduction of "writing words and code for bitcoin" isn't too accurate anymore. I guess it's all words now.

I care about people, I care about ideas, and I care about freedom of speech. The Logos, if you will. I care about freedom and prosperity, which is why I care about the inalienable property rights that bitcoin represents. I care about privacy, encryption, truth, beauty, and free and open-source software.

I care about the internet, and what it used to represent. I hope that my kids will be able to enjoy a free and open internet, just like I used to enjoy. I hope that they can express themselves freely, without being forced to use their true names. I hope that they can escape the eternal doom-loop of cheap dopamine and endless car crashes that is the current iteration of the internet.

Where do we go from here? I don't know. I will do what I can to help bring about a better future. A more prosperous, more stable, more sane future. I am aware that technology isn't everything; it is necessary, but not sufficient. Just like cryptography: necessary, but not sufficient. The onus is on us. I believe it is in our power to build a better internet: an internet worth having.
